Development Timeline

First Steps

Project Alpha: The Beginning

My first foray into Unity. This was a simple 2D platformer where I learned the fundamentals of C#, physics, and the Unity Editor. It was a humble start, but it sparked my passion.

Game Jam

Chrono Glitch

Q3 2021

Developed in 48 hours for a game jam. A top-down puzzle game where players manipulate time. This project taught me the importance of scope management and rapid prototyping under pressure.

Shader Programming

Pixel Galaxy

Q1 2022

Dived into the world of shaders and procedural generation. Created a visually stunning space exploration prototype with custom lighting and particle effects. A huge step up in technical art skills.

Multiplayer Networking

Project Chimera

Q4 2022

Tackled the challenge of online multiplayer. Built a proof-of-concept for a cooperative survival game, learning about client-server architecture, state synchronization, and latency compensation.

Commercial Release

Whispering Woods

Q3 2023

My first commercially released indie title on Steam. A narrative-driven adventure game. This journey taught me about marketing, QA, community management, and the full development lifecycle.
